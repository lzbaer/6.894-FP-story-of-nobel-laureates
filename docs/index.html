The Stories of High-Achievers

Ameneh Fadaie , Austin M Parker , & Lisa Baer | 6.894 : Interactive Data Visualization | MIT

Final Project Milestone

Our Dataset

- Publication records of Nobel laureates in physics , chemistry , and medicine from 1900-2016

- NOBEL awarded papers in all categories: physics, chemistry, medicine, peace, economy, and literature.

- Include the laureate’s names, affiliation, published and prized year, laureates’ countries, their motivations, and their research.

Our Goal

* Determine what conditions and environments facilitate scientific creativity:

Telling the story of each laureate individually (Scrollytelling Visualization) :

- Their motivations and personalities

- Their locations (Stay in their birth town or move)

- Solo research or collaborative?
